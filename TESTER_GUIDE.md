## 2. What to go through

Tick each one as you do it. If anything goes wrong at any step, stop and report it.

**Getting in**
- [ ] Open the site. Does it load quickly? Does anything look broken or cut off?
- [ ] Sign up with a new account
- [ ] Receive the verification code / email and complete signup
- [ ] Sign out, then sign back in
- [ ] Try signing in with the **wrong** password — does it tell you clearly what's wrong?
- [ ] Try "forgot password" if it exists

**Ordering food**
- [ ] Browse the menu. Do all photos load? Any missing or blurry images?
- [ ] Search for a dish
- [ ] Open a dish and read its description and price
- [ ] Add items to the cart, including one with options/extras if available
- [ ] Change the quantity in the cart
- [ ] Remove an item from the cart
- [ ] Leave the site and come back — is your cart still there?

**Checkout & payment**
- [ ] Enter a delivery address
- [ ] Check the total: do item prices + delivery + tax actually add up correctly?
- [ ] Pay with the test card above
- [ ] Do you get a confirmation on screen?
- [ ] Do you get a confirmation email?

**After ordering**
- [ ] Find your order in the orders/history page
- [ ] Try the order tracking page
- [ ] Check your account page — is your name, email and address correct?
- [ ] Try updating something in your account and refresh to confirm it saved

**Everything else**
- [ ] Click every link in the top menu and the footer (Help, Privacy, Terms, etc.)
- [ ] Does anything lead to a blank page or an error?

## 3. Things worth deliberately trying

Real customers make mistakes, so please make some on purpose:

- Submit a form with fields left **empty** — does it explain what's missing?
- Type a fake email like `abc@abc` — is it rejected politely?
- Enter a huge quantity, like 999 of one dish
- Press the pay/submit button **twice quickly** — do you get charged or ordered twice?
- Press the browser **back button** in the middle of checkout
- Turn your phone **sideways** — does the layout still look right?
- Turn off your internet for a moment mid-action — does it crash, or show a sensible message?

---

## 4. What counts as a bug

Report it if:

- Something doesn't work at all (button does nothing, page won't load)
- You see an error message, or a page in a strange language like `undefined` or `NaN`
- The price or total is wrong
- Text is cut off, overlapping, or too small to read
- Something takes **more than ~3 seconds** and gives you no sign it's working
- You got confused about what to do next
- Something looks fine on your computer but broken on your phone (or the reverse)

"It felt confusing" is a **valid and useful** bug. Please report it.

---

## 5. How to report

Fill the form with:

1. **What you were trying to do** — e.g. "add butter chicken to my cart"
2. **What you did, step by step** — e.g. "opened menu → tapped butter chicken → tapped Add"
3. **What you expected** — "it should go into the cart"
4. **What actually happened** — "nothing happened, the button just greyed out"
5. **Phone or computer, and which browser**
6. **A screenshot or screen recording** — this helps us more than anything else

📸 **Please always attach a screenshot if you can.**

- iPhone: press Side button + Volume Up
- Android: press Power + Volume Down
- Windows: press `Windows key + Shift + S`
- Mac: press `Shift + Command + 4`

One report per problem, please — it's easier for us to track.
